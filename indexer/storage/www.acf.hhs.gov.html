Durbin Feeling Languages Act Survey

We are seeking additional input through listening sessions on the design and content of the survey. ANA is considering options for sending the survey to Native governments and stakeholders representing Native Hawaiian and Pacific Island communities pursuant to the law, such as sending the survey directly to the tribal or Pacific Island government. Please provide feedback on the best approach to survey your Native speaker community.

Learn more about the Durbin Feeling Languages Act

MMIP Podcast Series

To bolster prevention efforts to mitigate the Missing and Murdered Indigenous Persons (MMIP) epidemic, the Administration for Native Americans (ANA), in collaboration with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Secretary’s Interdepartmental Council on Native American Affairs (ICNAA), developed a podcast series to raise awareness, provide prevention resources and educate listeners on MMIP.
